  • The Sandsnakes Win First Anual Dodgeball Tournament

    The Sandsnakes, a team made up of five female seniors at Holly High School, took home the title at the first anual Holiday Dodgeball Tournament on December 6th. They showed a dominating performance and defeated all other contenders, winning themselves each a $25 gift card to The Red Devil.

  • Trick Or Treat Event: Fun For the Whole Family

    The Holly Community Center hosted its first annual Family Trick-Or-Treat Night on October 31st. Local businesses set up booths and distributed candy to all the children in attendance. There was also a costume contest and bobbing for apples among other events. Everyone agreed that it was a great time and many commented that they are already looking forward to next year.

  • Fall Craft Show Is a Big Hit

    The thirty-seventh anual Holly Art and Crafts show went off without a hitch on November 1st. Hundreds were in attendance and much money was raised for two local nonprofits: The Holly Historical Society's Hadley House Museum and Holly High School's Project Graduation. It was a fun time for all ages and featured art from dozens of local artists.
